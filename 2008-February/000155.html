Summary: iocharset=utf8

Details: As far as I can tell there's no way to pass the utf-8 option to mount.cifs. So utf-8 files on the remote system show up as iso-8859-1 files when mounted with smb4k.  This causes problems with k3b and other software.

I can manually mount the share with iocharset=utf-8, and it works fine, so it should just be a matter of passing the option.  I looked under custom options but the options available there are very limited. Would you consider adding this one?
