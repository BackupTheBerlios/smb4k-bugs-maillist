By: dustpuppy

Comment:
I guess the reason why you do not see the shares is that your Windows box running ME. Smb4K's default setting is to automatically detect the protocol when querying the remote host for its shares. Most of the time this method chooses the RPC protocol. But as far as I can remember, Windows ME (and older versions) needs the RAP protocol. Have you tried setting the RAP protocol either in the custom options dialog or in the configuration dialog (Samba->Utility Programs->net)?
