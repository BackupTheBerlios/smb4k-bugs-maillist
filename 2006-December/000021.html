Summary: IP's for bookmarked not updated when network is rescaned

Details: Today, the IP of a machine on our network changed.  I went to mount it, and was given a failture message, telling me that there was no route to the IP.  OK, that makes sense, but why wasn't the network rescaned periodically?

Next, I told it to rescan the network.  I was then able to click on the tree, expand that machine, and double click on the drive (G$ in this case) and mount it.  However, if I went to the bookmark for that computer to mount that same drive, it was telling me that there was no route to that IP, and it was using the same (old) IP.

1. It seems that rescanning the network does not update the IP globally.
2. It seems that Smb4K needs to rescan the network periodically.
