Summary: Bookmark a share from the Mounted Shares view

Details: There's one issue open for me, which I missed even in smb4k for KDE3 the whole time: If I mount a share and leave the "Add to bookmarks" checkbox unchecked, it does not appear in bookmarks - so far it's ok and understandable. I missed the feature of adding an already mounted share to bookmarks, or I haven't seen it clearly, maybe ;-)

In the latest smb4k-0.10-beta I see that there could be such a possibility, but it's not absolutely clearly handled.

>From the "Mounted Shares" view with one of the mounted shares selected I have an inactive menu entry "Bookmarks - Add bookmark" (which maybe has a different meaning of creating a totally new bookmark, who knows, that's why I added this as a wish and not as a bug). Nevertheless, it's not clear to me why this menu entry is not active at this point.

>From intuition I would expect a different menu here, like:
- Add new bookmark...
  Opens a dialog for a completely new share.
- Add (current) to bookmarks
  Adds the currently mounted share to bookmarks without any dialog,

Furthermore, it would be intuitive to have an "Add to bookmarks" menu entry in the context menu over the share, too.
