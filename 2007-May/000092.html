This access is really due to smb4k; I have verified that no other software started in the session asks for wallet access.
Anyway, smb4k hangs waiting for the response from a DCOP call during this time, and fails to mount any of my shares.

Comment:
OK, here is a preliminary report: I was able to reproduce the problem with version 0.8.3 and Smb4K behaved like stated in the bug report. 

Looking at the API docs, I noticed that the smb4k.desktop file was lacking the 'X-DCOP-ServiceType=Unique' entry that is needed, however, because of Smb4K being a KUniqueApplication. After adding it to the end of the desktop file, I wasn't able to reproduce the hanging anymore. Maybe you could pass this on to the reporter and ask him if it also fixes his problem.
