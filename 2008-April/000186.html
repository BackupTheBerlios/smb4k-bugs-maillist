I use smb4k parallely to automounter on my system. If I mount one and the same share //computer/share parallely by automounter for instance to /mnt/cifs/computer/share and by smb4k to $HOME/smb4k/computer/share the share is shown twice in the share viewer in smb4k.
The one from the automounter is greyed out in the viewer, and on opening it I get the contents of the directory /mnt in a file browser.
I guess, this is not the desired behaviour.

Comment:
That each mount is shown is desired behavior. That it is greyed out is in this case also OK, because Smb4K recognises it as a "foreign" mount, because it is neither mounted under your home directory nor under the default mount prefix. I have to admit that Smb4K could try harder to figure out whether the mount is indeed owned by a different user.

That a wrong path is opened in Konqueror is undesired. A fix will be delivered with version 0.9.4.
