Date: 2008-Apr-09 11:31
By: dustpuppy

Comment:
That's strange, because I only applied two changes to the mounter. The first one is the fix for the incorrect path of mount points where either "cifs" or "smbfs" occurs in the name, and the other one is that the mounter now uses its own timer through the startTimer()/timerEvent() combo instead of a connection to the now deprecated Smb4KGlobal::timer(). I did not touch the code of the smb4k_mount utility.

In order to reproduce the freezing of your computer, I installed autofs on several Debian unstable machines. I haven't experienced anything abnormal until now, especially no freezes or kernel oopses.
